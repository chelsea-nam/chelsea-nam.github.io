Hi there! Our research group in the Department of Earth, Ocean, and Atmospheric Sciences at Florida State University. The group's current research includes:

  • Tropical Cyclone Genesis and Intensification
  • Tropical Cyclone Microphysics
  • Compound Hazards induced by Tropical Cyclones

To better understand the complex, multi-scale mechanisms around tropical cyclones, we use a number of different datasets and methods including radar (airborne, shipborne and land-based) data, ensemble high-resolution mesoscale models (WRF and CM1) and large-scale reanalyses.
